Projects

Metaporter

This is a handheld 3D scanning tool that uses multi-sensor data and neural networks to generate accurate models of still objects. The quality of the 3D models depends on the quality of the data collected. Users interact with the device through a keypad matrix and view the reconstruction on a host machine.

Mock Reddit Website

This is a mock social media website that enables users to create accounts, post, upvote/downvote, and view a feed of posts from all users. It provides a platform for people to share and discover new perspectives on various topics with basic discussion board functionalities.

Rogue

This is a terminal-based adventure game where players act as a knight trying to reach the end of a maze. They must collect objects and fight monsters to succeed. The levels are different each time, depending on the selected map. The game provides a challenging and thrilling experience.
